Population

Lima's 2024 population is now estimated at 11,361,938. In 1950, the population of Lima was 1,065,888. Lima has grown by 157,556 in the last year, which represents a 1.41% annual change.

Geographic

Lima, Peru's capital, blends history, culture, and coastal beauty. Explore the UNESCO-listed historic center, vibrant neighborhoods like Miraflores and Barranco, and indulge in the world-class culinary scene. Relax on picturesque beaches and enjoy stunning ocean views. Lima offers a captivating blend of old-world charm and modern excitement.
