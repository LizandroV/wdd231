Site Name:

FlavorFind

The name FlavorFind reflects the app's purpose of helping users discover new and exciting recipes full of flavor. It's short, catchy, and easy to remember, making it perfect for branding. The name also hints at the app's search functionality, emphasizing the ease of finding the perfect dish.

Purpose

FlavorFind is designed to be a one-stop hub for food lovers who want to explore, search, and save delicious recipes. The app pulls data from the Spoonacular API to provide users with a daily top 15 recipe list, powerful search options, and the ability to save favorite recipes in localStorage for easy access. The responsive design ensures an excellent experience on both mobile and desktop devices.

Scenarios

"How can I find quick and easy dinner recipes using the ingredients I already have at home?"

"Can I save my favorite recipes to easily access them later without creating an account?"
